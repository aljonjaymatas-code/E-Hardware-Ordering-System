About E-Hardware

E-Hardware is an innovative online hardware ordering system designed to make the process of browsing, ordering, and managing hardware tools and construction materials simpler and faster. Our goal is to digitalize the hardware store experience for both business owners and customers.

Our Mission

To provide a modern, efficient, and reliable system that bridges the gap between traditional hardware operations and digital technology — making it easier for anyone to order hardware supplies anytime, anywhere.

Our Vision

We envision a future where every hardware store operates digitally, giving customers real-time access to products, inventory, and services — leading to a more organized and productive community of builders and suppliers.

System Features

  • 🔍 Real-Time Inventory: View available items instantly without logging in.
  • 🧾 Order Management: Process and track customer orders with ease.
  • 💳 Secure Transactions: Safe and transparent purchase processing.
  • 📊 User-Friendly Interface: Simple navigation for admins and customers alike.

Why Choose E-Hardware?

E-Hardware combines the convenience of online shopping with the reliability of traditional hardware systems. It is built for speed, accuracy, and simplicity, ensuring every transaction is smooth and every product is up-to-date.

Developer

This system was developed by Aljonjay Matas using Java (Backend), MySQL (Database), and HTML/CSS/JavaScript (Frontend) as part of a comprehensive E-Hardware Management System project.
